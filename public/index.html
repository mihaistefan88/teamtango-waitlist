Frequently asked questions

What is TeamTango?

TeamTango is an all-in-one team engagement and culture platform. Teams use it to share highlights and updates on a team wall, give kudos with @mentions and reactions, celebrate birthdays and work anniversaries automatically, request and approve time off, and track engagement with built-in insights.

Who is TeamTango for?

TeamTango is built for remote, hybrid, and in-office teams that care about culture — from startups to mid-size companies. Team leads use it to keep recognition visible, HR and people-ops teams use it for time-off management and engagement insights, and everyone uses the team wall to stay connected.

What features does TeamTango include?

TeamTango includes a team wall with smart post categorization (highlights, kudos, challenges), peer recognition with @mentions and emoji reactions, automated birthday and work anniversary celebrations, PTO and time-off management with approval workflows and a team calendar, engagement analytics, and visual org charts with a searchable team directory.

How is TeamTango different from a chat tool like Slack or Teams?

Chat tools are built for fast, ephemeral conversation — recognition and milestones scroll away within hours. TeamTango is a dedicated culture layer that complements chat: kudos, celebrations, and team highlights live on a persistent team wall, time off is managed with proper approval workflows, and engagement is measurable instead of invisible.

When does TeamTango launch and how do I get access?
